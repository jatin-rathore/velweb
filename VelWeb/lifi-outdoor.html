LiFi Outdoor Solutions

Extending the speed and security of LiFi to open environments.

What is LiFi Outdoor?

LiFi Outdoor refers to the deployment of light-based wireless communication systems in external environments like streets, parking lots, industrial sites, defense zones, and transportation systems.

Unlike indoor systems, LiFi Outdoor is designed to operate reliably across longer distances and in variable lighting and weather conditions using focused beam or infrared-based communication.

Advantages of LiFi Outdoor

  • Secure point-to-point communication in open spaces
  • Immune to radio frequency interference
  • Ideal for defense, surveillance, and industrial automation
  • High bandwidth and low-latency in outdoor line-of-sight environments

Use Cases

  • Military communication in RF-sensitive zones
  • Smart traffic systems and vehicle-to-infrastructure (V2X)
  • Industrial automation in outdoor manufacturing facilities
  • Private high-speed links between buildings or data centers
