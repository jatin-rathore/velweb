Li-Fi Dongle & Access Point

Li-Fi Dongle

A Li-Fi dongle is a USB-powered receiver that enables laptops and other non-Li-Fi-native devices to connect to a Li-Fi network. It receives modulated light signals and converts them back into digital data for the user. Plug-and-play, compact, and fast â€” itâ€™s your portable gateway to light-speed internet.

Li-Fi Access Point

The Li-Fi access point is a smart LED light source that acts as a data transmitter. It modulates light to send data to compatible Li-Fi devices or dongles. It functions like a Wi-Fi router but uses light instead of radio frequencies, enabling high-speed, secure, and interference-free communication indoors.
