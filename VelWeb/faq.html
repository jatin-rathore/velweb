Frequently Asked Questions

What is Li-Fi?

Li-Fi (Light Fidelity) is a wireless communication technology that uses visible light to transmit data at high speeds, offering a secure and interference-free alternative to Wi-Fi.

How is Li-Fi different from Wi-Fi?

While Wi-Fi uses radio waves, Li-Fi uses light waves, which allows for faster data transmission and improved security since light cannot penetrate walls.

Where can Li-Fi be used?

Li-Fi is ideal for use in hospitals, aircraft, industrial settings, and any environment where RF communication may pose risks or limitations.

Is Li-Fi safe for humans?

Yes. Li-Fi uses light sources such as LEDs, which are safe and already widely used for illumination.

Does Li-Fi require line-of-sight?

Li-Fi performs best with direct line-of-sight but can still function through reflections on surfaces, depending on the environment and receiver sensitivity.
