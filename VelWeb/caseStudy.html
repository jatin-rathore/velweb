Case Study: Li-Fi Deployment in Smart Factory

Enhancing industrial communication through high-speed, light-based wireless networks.

Overview

Velmenni partnered with a leading manufacturing facility to implement India‚Äôs first industrial-grade Li-Fi network. The goal was to overcome wireless interference from metal structures and create a secure, efficient communication backbone.

Challenge

Traditional Wi-Fi networks faced performance drops due to interference from heavy machinery and metallic infrastructure. The client also required a solution immune to RF-based espionage.

Our Solution

Velmenni deployed a custom Li-Fi mesh network across the factory floor. Light-based communication provided stable bandwidth even in interference-heavy zones. We used directional light transmitters integrated into overhead fixtures.

Results

  • 4x increase in stable connectivity across the shop floor.
  • Zero signal loss due to RF interference.
  • Enhanced security due to line-of-sight transmission.
  • 20% improvement in data transfer efficiency between IoT sensors and control systems.
